---Driver
Y button: Zeros gyro
Left Bumper: robot Centric(forward is robots forward)
B button: Speaker Tracking

---Operator
X Button: Launcher Down
Y Button: Launcher Up
A Button: Activate Feed
B Button: Cancle Intake and Feed 

Left Bumper: Intake Assembly 
Left Trigger: Reverse Intake and Feed 
Right Bumper: Launcher Tracking + Launcher Speed 
Right Triger: Amp Launch Speed 

Back Button: Backwards Angle For Speaker

D-Pad Down: Launcher Down to ~Zero
D-Pad Left: Safety Launch Angle 
D-Pad Right: Speaker Up close Angle 
D-Pad Up: Amp Launch Angle
